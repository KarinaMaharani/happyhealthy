Medical Disclaimer & Terms of Use

1. Not a Substitute for Professional Medical Advice

Happy Healthy is a drug interaction checker and mental health symptom tracking tool. This application is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition or medication.

2. Drug Interaction Information

Drug interaction data is sourced from DrugBank database. While we strive for accuracy:

  • Information may be incomplete or outdated
  • Absence of interaction data does NOT mean drugs are safe to combine
  • Individual reactions may vary based on your health condition
  • ALWAYS consult a healthcare provider before starting, stopping, or changing medications

3. Mental Health Symptom Tracking

Symptom tracking features are for informational purposes only:

  • This is NOT a diagnostic tool
  • Symptom categories are based on WHO ICD-10 classifications
  • If you experience suicidal thoughts or self-harm urges, seek immediate professional help
  • Emergency contacts: National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (988), Emergency Services (911)

4. Caregiver-Patient Relationship

If you use caregiver monitoring features:

  • Patients must explicitly approve caregiver access
  • Caregivers can view symptom records and drug searches of approved patients
  • Caregivers are notified of concerning symptoms (severity 3+, suicidal thoughts, self-harm)
  • This does NOT replace professional mental health care

5. Privacy & Data

By using Happy Healthy:

  • Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties
  • You can delete your account and data at any time
  • Caregivers only see data from approved patient relationships

6. Limitation of Liability

Happy Healthy and its developers are NOT liable for:

  • Any medical decisions made based on information from this app
  • Adverse drug reactions or interactions
