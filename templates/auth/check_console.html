How to find your verification link:

  2. Find the email output (starts with "Content-Type: text/plain")
  3. Copy the verification URL (looks like: http://127.0.0.1:8000/auth/verify-email/...)
  4. Paste it in your browser

Note: In production, emails will be sent to your actual email address. For development, we use console output to avoid needing email server configuration.
